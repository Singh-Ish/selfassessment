Instructions to access the portal:

  1. To access Self assessment website you need to be on Carleton Network
  2. Student have to use their cmail email account to login to the we application
      -sample : "****@cmail.carleton.ca"
  3. Once you submit the login request, an email will to be sent to the registered Id
    • If you think you are an authorised user but still can't login kindly contact to Admin staff to have access to the portal
    • No email will be sent to unauthorized Email Id's
  4. It uses a token based validation and the token will be valid for 10 minutes. If the token expires you have to request it again
  5. For students after succcessfully login to the portal, they will be redirected to the Student dashboard which will have all the information about them and their respective groups
    • If there is any incorrect information , please reach out to Admin staff to address the issue before submitting the assessment
    • You can also share your feedback about the portal at the end of assessment
